Our Story
We believe food should be delicious, accessible, and most importantly, eaten with good friends. At our modest restaurant on the river, you only have to bring the friends. Come as you are: after a long day, before the big game, passing through to the next adventure. Our chef, R. Ruby, has created a menu of American street food with an elevated twist. Please warm up by the fire in our cozy dining area or cool off on our patio with the Mississippi river breeze during a hot summer evening. We have an extensive wine and seasonal craft beer list all meant to be enjoyed in a shareable bottle or growler. Give us a call or submit your contact information to inquire about our catering options for small and large events.
